How do I edit my name or my e-mail address?

If you have "Travel mode" or "Internet" as your call setup, you can't receive a call. But to make your life easier, the app will automatically switch your call setup to "Local call" for you to be able to receive it.

  • From the main screen, you will find the recent contacts you have called. If icon is displayed then tap on the contact or tap  and swipe left.
  • If you have recently made a Call to a contact it will show "Calling" under the contact's name. Just tap on that contact to initiate a Call. Your call will be connected depending on your call setup.
