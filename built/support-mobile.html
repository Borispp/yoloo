Yoloo support

Calling Questions

How can I check my call history from the app?

If you don't see the icon: Yoloo is not running. Restart it from the Applications folder (Mac), Start Menu (Windows), or Programs Menu (Linux). When prompted, sign in with the email and password for your Yoloo account. Your computer will begin to sync with your Yoloo account online.

Can I receive an support service call?

Verify that your number is correct and click on "Send me a new PIN". We will then send you a new pin to your phone. Enter that new pin to log in to your account.

How do I change the language on my account?

The call is placed through your mobile operator. Your call will be connected through a local number and connect you to your international number.
